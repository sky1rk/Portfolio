About Me

I am a 3rd-year BS Computer Science student at West Visayas State University with a strong interest in game development and digital editing. My academic background has provided me with a solid foundation in programming and problem-solving, while my passion for creative design drives me to explore the intersection of technology and storytelling. I aspire to contribute to the game development industry by creating engaging, interactive experiences. In my free time, I enjoy watching dramas and listening to music, which often inspire new perspectives and ideas for my work.
